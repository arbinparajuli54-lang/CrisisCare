Community Help & Donations

This space connects volunteers, donors, and coordinators so that help can reach people in need as quickly and safely as possible.

How you can help

Donate Essentials

Support relief efforts by donating food, water, clothing, blankets, medicines, and hygiene kits through verified collection centers.

  • Check expiry dates of all food and medicine.
  • Label items clearly (e.g., “baby food”, “blankets”).
  • Sort clothing by size and type if possible.

Volunteer Your Time

Join as a volunteer to help with packing, distribution, first aid, counselling, or digital coordination and awareness.

  • On‑ground relief work and logistics.
  • Medical and psychological support.
  • Online information and helpline support.

Support Financially

Contribute funds to trusted organizations running verified campaigns for affected families and rebuilding efforts.

  • Prefer official or government‑linked campaigns.
  • Keep transaction receipts and confirmations safely.
  • Be alert for online fraud and scams.
